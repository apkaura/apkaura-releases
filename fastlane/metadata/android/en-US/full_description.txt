Apkaura is the browser for APK users — people who install apps outside the Play Store from APKMirror, F-Droid, independent developers, and open-source communities.

At only 7 MB and requiring no Google Play Services, Apkaura runs on any Android 7.0 or newer device, including GMS-less phones and custom ROMs.

Every APK is verified before install, no matter where it comes from. Point Apkaura at any APK on your device — a file manager, a Telegram or chat attachment, a browser download, or a share from another app — and the built-in APK Inspector checks the signature, audits requested permissions, and runs a VirusTotal scan on every APK, XAPK, APKS, and APKM file before you install it. Optional Shizuku integration enables silent installs without repeated system prompts.

Additional features include private tabs that stay off the recent-apps screen, ad and tracker blocking, and a distraction-free Reader Mode with light, sepia, and dark themes.
